mapshakers download centre

Vector and raster data are distributed under CC BY-SA 4.0 license, unless otherwise specified.

GeoTiffs are rendered in web mercator projection - epsg:3857 and compressed by LZW algorithm. GeoTiffs or BigTiffs contains 512x512px tiles.

Most of the maps are derived from awesome project OSM, do not forget to mention the source: mapshakers & OpenStreetMap & contributors!
